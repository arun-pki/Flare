Magnification

Magnification changes how large or small your drawing appears without changing its dimensions.

Magnify

Select an object to keep in view as you magnify the drawing.
If you do not select an object, the last object drawn is the center point of the magnification.
Select an option below:
To increase the magnification, click on Magnify button on toolbar.
To reduce the magnification, click on Reduce button on toolbar.
